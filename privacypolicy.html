Privacy Policy - Scan Sequencer

Last updated: August 23, 2025

TL;DR: Your camera data never leaves your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information. This is a completely client-side application that runs in your browser.

What is Scan Sequencer?

Scan Sequencer is an interactive music creation tool that uses your camera to generate real-time music. It analyzes pixel color changes from your webcam feed to trigger musical notes, creating a virtual keyboard experience.

Camera and Video Data

Complete Privacy: All camera and video processing happens entirely within your web browser on your local device. Here's what this means:

  • No Data Transmission: Your camera feed is never sent to any servers or external services
  • Local Processing Only: All image analysis and music generation occurs on your device
  • No Recording: The application does not record, save, or store any video or image data
  • No Facial Recognition: We do not perform any facial recognition or biometric analysis
  • No Screenshots: The application does not capture or save still images

Data We Don't Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or process any of the following:

  • Personal information (names, emails, addresses)
  • Camera or video data
  • Audio recordings
  • Biometric or facial data
  • Location information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies for tracking purposes

Technical Information

The application is built using:

  • P5.js: For camera access and visual processing
  • Tone.js: For audio synthesis and music generation
  • Client-side JavaScript: All processing runs in your browser

Cookies

We use minimal cookies only for:

  • Cookie consent notification (to remember your cookie preferences)
  • No tracking, analytics, or advertising cookies are used

You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting the core functionality of Scan Sequencer.

Third-Party Services

This application may be hosted on platforms like GitHub Pages, which may have their own privacy policies. However, the Scan Sequencer application itself does not integrate with any third-party services for data collection or analytics.

Browser Permissions

The application requests camera access solely to:

  • Capture real-time video feed for color analysis
  • Generate music based on pixel color changes
  • Provide the interactive music experience

You can revoke camera permissions at any time through your browser settings.

Open Source

This project is open source, which means:

  • You can inspect all code to verify our privacy claims
  • The source code is available on GitHub
  • There are no hidden data collection mechanisms

Children's Privacy

This application is suitable for all ages. Since we don't collect any personal data, there are no special considerations for children's privacy under COPPA or similar regulations.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since we don't collect contact information, we cannot notify users directly of changes.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the application, you can:

  • View the source code on GitHub
  • Submit issues or questions through the GitHub repository

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store any personal data:

  • There's no data to access, modify, or delete
  • You have complete control over your camera permissions
  • You can use the application completely anonymously

Bottom Line: Scan Sequencer is designed with privacy by default. Your creative sessions remain completely private and local to your device.
